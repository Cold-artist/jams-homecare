Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At Jams Homecare ("we", "our", "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our home healthcare services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide safe and effective healthcare services:

  • Personal Information: Name, age, gender, mobile number, email address, and residential address.
  • Health Information: Medical history, symptoms, prescriptions, and lab test results required for diagnosis or treatment.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information when accessing our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To schedule and conduct home visits (Doctors, Nurses, Lab Technicians).
  • To deliver lab reports and prescriptions via Email or WhatsApp.
  • To communicate booking confirmations, reminders, and service updates.
  • To process payments and generate invoices.
  • To improve our service quality based on feedback.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Medical Professionals: With our staff doctors/nurses strictly for your treatment.
  • Partner Labs: With NABL-accredited diagnostic labs to process your samples (only necessary details).
  • Legal Compliance: If required by Indian law or legal authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All digital records are stored on secure servers, and sensitive medical information is handled with strict confidentiality by our staff.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your medical records, correct any inaccuracies, or request deletion of your account (subject to medical record retention laws).
