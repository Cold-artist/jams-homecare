Refund & Cancellation Policy

Fair and Transparent Policies

We strive to provide reliable and punctual service. However, we understand that plans change. Our cancellation and refund guidelines are as follows:

1. Cancellation Policy

  • Free Cancellation: You may cancel your booking free of charge up to 2 hours before the scheduled appointment time.
  • Late Cancellation: Cancellations made within 2 hours of the scheduled time may attract a nominal convenience fee of ₹100 if our staff has already been dispatched.

2. Refund Policy

Refunds are applicable only for pre-paid bookings in the following cases:

  • Service Not Delivered: If our staff fails to reach your location.
  • Cancellation by Jams Homecare: If we cancel the booking due to staff unavailability or unforeseen circumstances.
  • Quality Issues: If a lab sample is rejected/hemolyzed and a re-sample cannot be collected.

Note: No refunds are provided for services already rendered (e.g., after nursing care is given or sample is collected).

3. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days and credited back to the original payment source.

4. Rescheduling

You can reschedule your appointment free of charge at any time, subject to slot availability. Please contact our support team at +91 97589 28213.
