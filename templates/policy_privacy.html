1. Introduction

Welcome to Jams Homecare ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use your data.

We collect information necessary to provide home healthcare services, including:

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL) and secure servers to protect your data. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
